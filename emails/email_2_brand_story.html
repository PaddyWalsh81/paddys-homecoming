⛰️ Born in County Carlow

Flying Tumbler takes its name from Mount Leinster — a 3,000ft peak in southeast Ireland where the wind is fierce enough to tumble anything airborne. The whiskey is made here, in the rolling countryside of County Carlow, where the barley grows thick and the water runs cold.

🍷 The Liquid

The Bird is a blend of single malt and single grain Irish whiskey, aged a minimum of 5 years in bourbon casks. Bottled at 44% ABV and non-chill filtered — meaning nothing is stripped out for appearance. What you taste is exactly what the distiller intended.

Smooth enough to sip neat. Bold enough to stand tall in a cocktail. Honest enough to not need a fancy story to cover for the liquid — though it has a great one anyway.
