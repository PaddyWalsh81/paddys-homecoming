3 ways to drink The Bird
*|FNAME|*, while Paddy plots his route home, here's what to do with the bottle. Three serves — one takes effort, one takes ice, one takes patience.
The Paddy's Old Fashioned
INGREDIENTS
2 oz The Bird
0.5 oz simple syrup
2 dashes Angostura
Orange peel
METHOD
Stir in a mixing glass, strain over a large ice cube, express the orange peel over the top.
The Homecoming Highball
INGREDIENTS
2 oz The Bird
4 oz ginger ale
Squeeze of lime
Fresh mint
METHOD
Fill a tall glass with ice, pour the whiskey, top with ginger ale, garnish with lime and mint.
The Bird, Neat
INGREDIENTS
2 oz The Bird
A few drops of water
No ice
METHOD
Glencairn or rocks glass, add the water, let it breathe, sip slowly. At 43% and non-chill filtered, you get the full story in every glass.
