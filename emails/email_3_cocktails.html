You’ve entered the sweepstakes. You know the story. Now let’s talk about how to actually enjoy The Bird. Here are three serves — from classic to dead simple.

🍻 The Paddy’s Old Fashioned

Ingredients

2 oz The Bird
0.5 oz simple syrup
2 dashes Angostura bitters
Orange peel

Method

Combine whiskey, syrup, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Express the orange peel and drop it in.

🍼 The Homecoming Highball

Ingredients

2 oz The Bird
4 oz ginger ale
Squeeze of lime
Sprig of fresh mint

Method

Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in The Bird, top with ginger ale. Squeeze in the lime and drop it in. Slap the mint between your palms to release the oils and nestle it on top.

🥃 The Bird Neat

Ingredients

2 oz The Bird
A few drops of water
No ice
That’s it.

Method

Pour into a Glencairn or rocks glass. Add a few drops of water to open it up. Let it breathe for a moment. Then sip slowly. At 44% and non-chill filtered, you get the full story in every glass.
